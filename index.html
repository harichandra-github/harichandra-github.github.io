My Journey

Born in Chalisgaon, Maharashtra, I started with Mechanical Engineering, but my real love was always software and automation. After graduation, I completed industrial training at QSpiders in Manual Testing, SQL, and Automation basics. Later, I upskilled at Scaler, mastering Data Structures, Low-Level Design (LLD), and Software Design Principles, guiding my design of robust and scalable automation frameworks. My professional journey led me to fintech and Salesforce domains, where I architect frameworks at Smallcase Technologies to automate real-user logins, end-to-end order flows, and multi-platform testing. Today, my work blends engineering precision, automation expertise, and curiosity for AI and emerging tech.
